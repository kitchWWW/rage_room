Rage Room invites participants to walk into a sonic space to explore despair, fury, and desperation. No person can constantly retain the moniker of good and we are all capable of destruction in some way shape or form. In this piece, grab your metaphorical weapon of choice and do what you have to do.

CW: Violence, Gore, Self-Harm, Body Horror

If any of these subjects are difficult to engage with, please feel free to participate as a listener only or remove yourself from the space until the piece is complete (approx. 8 minutes).

Your answers will not be saved, recorded, or shared at any time during or after the piece. While honest answers are encouraged, dishonest answers are fine if you would like to participate fully without causing yourself distress.
